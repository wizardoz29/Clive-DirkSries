Hi lover of books!, this is an article that lists out the books of the great Clive Cussler in the Dirk Pitt series.

Clive Cussler is an american who has established himself as a legend in writing Maritime novels. He is the author of many great bestsellers of his time. His writing niche is mainly on maritime and adventure.

He was also an underwater explorer. His thriller novels, many featuring the character Dirk Pitt, have made The New York Times fiction best-seller list 17+ times. He is also the founder of NUMA.

Most popular and tipped to be the best series out of many is the one of Dirk Pitt. Dirk is an adventurer and the assistant director of NUMA (National Underwater Marine Agency). With his bestfriend, the Italian Al Giordino, the two formed a formidable duo that pulled off impossible tasks time and over.
